Accessible Rooms

Accessible rooms are thoughtfully designed to ensure a comfortable stay for guests with disabilities. Here's what's included:

- Accessible queen-size bed with lower height for easier access
- Wide doorways and spacious layout for easy maneuverability
- Roll-in shower or accessible bathtub with grab bars and a handheld showerhead
- Raised toilet seat with grab bars
- Lowered light switches and other controls
- Visual and audible emergency alarms
- Accessible closet and wardrobe with lower hanging rods
Every detail is carefully crafted to ensure inclusivity and comfort.
